Anal itching is a common condition. The itch in or around the anus is often intense and can be embarrassing and uncomfortable.Anal itching, also called pruritus ani (proo-RIE-tus A-nie), has several possible causes. They include infections, hemorrhoids and ongoing diarrhea. Skin inflammation, also called dermatitis, is another cause.If the symptoms don't clear up with self-care, talk with your health care provider. With treatment, most people get complete relief.Symptoms of anal itching may include intense itching, inflammation, burning and soreness. The itching and irritation may be short lived or more persistent, depending on the cause. Anal itching often is worse at bedtime or in hot, humid weather.Medical care isn't needed for most anal itching.
