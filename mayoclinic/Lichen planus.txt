Lichen planus (LIE-kun PLAY-nus) is a condition of the skin, hair, nails, mouth and genitals. On skin, lichen planus often appears as purple, itchy, flat bumps that develop over several weeks. In the mouth and genital mucosa, lichen planus forms lacy white patches, sometimes with painful sores.Mild lichen planus of the skin may not need treatment. If the condition causes pain or intense itching, you may need prescription medicine.Symptoms of lichen planus vary depending on the part of the body affected.
