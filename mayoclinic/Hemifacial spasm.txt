Hemifacial spasm is a nervous system condition in which the muscles on one side of the face twitch. The cause of hemifacial spasm is most often a blood vessel touching or pulsing against a facial nerve. A facial nerve injury or a tumor also can cause it. But over months to years, they occur almost all the time.Sometimes, hemifacial spasms occur on both sides of the face.
