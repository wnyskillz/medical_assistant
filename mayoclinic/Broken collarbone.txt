The collarbone, also known as the clavicle, connects the shoulder blade to the breastbone. Common causes of a broken collarbone include falls, sports and traffic accidents. Infants sometimes break their collarbones while being born.Seek medical help quickly for a broken collarbone. Most heal well with ice, pain relievers, a sling, physical therapy and time.
