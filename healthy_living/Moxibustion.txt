Moxibustion amanipulative and body based practiceis a therapy withintraditional Chinese medicine. Dried moxa herb a mugwort is burned usually just above but sometimes directly on the skin over acupuncture points . The herb may be in the form of incense sticks or wool .Moxibustion is used to treat conditions similar to those treated withacupunctureincluding pain headache and chronic fatigue .Moxibustion can cause circular burns which resemble burns from cigarette tips and blisters .See alsoOverview of Integrative Complementary and Alternative Medicine.
